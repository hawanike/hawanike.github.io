Internally Motivated

Without any pressure from parents, society, or culture, I strongly believe that life should be meaningful, otherwise why would we exist at all? Since young age, I always tried to find the meaning of life. And to be able to do so, along the way of finding the meaning, I must continue to develop myself in every aspects, so that once the meaning is found, I would be capable of it. Life offered me chances to give up but I consider them all as challenges to overcome, and the reward is a better me. Keeping on this momentum is crucial to the amount of effort I'll be putting in my goals and living in general.
