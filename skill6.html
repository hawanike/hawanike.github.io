Leadership

Leadership

Since socializing with others from kindergarten onwards, I've always ended up being at the leading role of whatever I do. Despite in later years trying to suppress it in order to give myself a break, my nature always took over. To describe it by words, I feel the necessity make sure that whatever I participate in goes well, and if it's not, I'm always the first to try optimizing it at all cost, whether in an orchestra or in classroom. With this trait naturally in me, my plan to materialize my vision is by starting it myself. And it's only possible if having capable teams of people doing it.
