Sports

Sport

As a child, I wasn't a very healthy person. I was allergic to many things and the way my parents chose to tackle this problem was to strengthen me through sports. I started from football and swimming at age 5, tennis and equestrian at age 8, badminton at age 10, basketball at age 13, archery at age 14 and a little bit of volleyball at 17. I enjoy playing all of them at anytime and since arriving at Constructor University in September 2022, I started playing volleyball for real and got into university's team with barely any experience at all.

More importantly, sports taught me to work as a team, to value effort and to be respectful to others. These side benefits are also instrumental to running my startup and establishing a proper working culture within the organization. This perfectly balanced my highly individualistic character and leadership.
